# ELEMENTS AND POWER, Print Edition Spec (KDP and Printify)

Product: ELEMENTS AND POWER, A 30 Day Activation Journal. Print companion to the digital PDF edition dated 2026 07 21 CT.

## Trim and bleed

* Trim size: 6 x 9 in (152.4 x 228.6 mm), the standard journal trim on both KDP and Printify.
* Bleed: 0.125 in on the three outer edges of any page whose background art reaches the edge. Since every interior page carries the cosmic navy background, the interior MUST be exported with bleed. Page size with bleed: 6.125 x 9.25 in (KDP adds bleed to width once, on the outside edge, and to height twice).
* Interior color: KDP premium color (or Printify standard color) is required because the pages are full navy. Black and white printing is not acceptable for this design. Alternative for a lower unit cost: a light interior variant (cream pages, navy text, rose accents) can be produced later as a second SKU.

## Interior margins (KDP minimums plus comfort)

* Outside margin: 0.5 in minimum. Recommended 0.625 in for this design so the rose border never risks the trim line.
* Top and bottom margins: 0.625 in recommended (0.25 in is the KDP floor, we stay well above it).
* Gutter (inside margin), driven by page count. At 61 to 150 pages KDP requires 0.625 in inside. Recommended gutter for this book: 0.75 in so the writing lines stay flat and usable.
* The rose frame used in the digital edition should be pulled in on the gutter side, or dropped on interior pages, so it never disappears into the binding.

## Page count, target 76 pages

The digital edition runs 39 pages. The print edition reaches the 70 to 80 page target by adding real writing space, which a paper journal needs anyway:

| Block | Pages |
|---|---|
| Title page | 1 |
| Copyright and edition page | 1 |
| Welcome | 1 |
| How to use this journal | 1 |
| Element dividers (5, each on a right hand page, blank ruled page on the back) | 10 |
| Day pages (30) | 30 |
| Ruled reflection page facing each day, one full lined page per day, 30 | 30 |
| Closing invitation page | 1 |
| Final ruled notes page | 1 |

Total: 76 pages. Page count must land on an even number; pad with ruled notes pages if the layout shifts. Keep dividers on right hand (odd, recto) pages.

## Spine width, white paper formula

KDP white paper: spine width in inches = page count x 0.002252.

* At 76 pages: 76 x 0.002252 = 0.1712 in.
* Note: KDP color books print on white paper, so 0.002252 is the correct multiplier. If the page count changes, recompute before building the cover.
* Spine text: KDP allows spine text only at 79 pages or more, so at 76 pages the spine stays art only (solid navy with a rose pinline). If pages grow to 80 or more, add ELEMENTS AND POWER in cream on the spine.

## Full wrap cover dimensions at 76 pages

Formula: cover width = bleed 0.125 + back 6 + spine + front 6 + bleed 0.125. Cover height = 9 + (2 x 0.125).

* Cover width: 12.25 + 0.1712 = 12.4212 in.
* Cover height: 9.25 in.
* At 300 dpi: 3726 x 2775 px (round up).
* Keep all text and the emblem at least 0.25 in inside the trim line, and clear of the barcode zone (KDP places a 2 x 1.2 in barcode at the lower right of the back cover).

## Copy laws for every print asset

No dash used as punctuation anywhere, use commas and periods. The zero cost word is banned, say gift. The subscription ending word beginning with c is banned, say stop. The change word beginning with t and ending in ormation is banned, say evolve or evolution. Full list lives in the LME brand rules.

## Export checklist

1. Interior PDF: 6.125 x 9.25 in pages with bleed, fonts embedded, flattened, PDF/X preferred.
2. Cover PDF: single full wrap file at the dimensions above, 300 dpi, CMYK preferred.
3. Recompute spine if page count moves, then rebuild the cover.
4. Order one proof copy before any public listing. Nothing goes live without Lizzy's explicit word.
