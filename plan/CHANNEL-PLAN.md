## 3. The Activate the Elements 30 Day Challenge

### 3.1 Mechanics
- 30 days, 5 elements, 6 days each: Earth days 1 to 6, Water 7 to 12, Fire 13 to 18, Air 19 to 24, Spirit 25 to 30.
- Each day has ONE small practice drawn from the science in the matching episode (example: Day 1, stand barefoot on grass for 5 minutes; Day 19, do 5 minutes of cyclic sighing).
- The **30 day journal** is the companion product: one spread per day with the practice, the study citation, a reflection prompt, and the declaration line.
- Completion moment: Day 30 finale episode invites viewers to post their declaration and tag the channel.

### 3.2 How shorts double as daily prompts
Every short IS the day's prompt. The hook teaches the study, the middle gives the practice, the close assigns it: "That is your Day [N] move." A viewer can binge the playlist as a course or follow along live day by day. Because episodes are numbered to challenge days, the playlist order and the journal order are identical, so nothing needs explaining twice.

### 3.3 End card and pinned comment strategy
- **End card (last 2 seconds of every short):** cosmic circle emblem, "Day [N] of 30, Activate the Elements," and the challenge playlist pointer. Shorts do not support clickable end screens, so the visual card plus the pinned comment carry the link work.
- **Pinned comment (every upload):** Day number, the practice in one line, the source citation, and the journal link ONLY once the listing is verified live. Until then: "Journal coming soon, follow so you do not miss it."
- **Comment engagement loop:** ask one question in every pinned comment ("Where will you ground today?"), heart early replies, pin a viewer declaration on finale days (6, 12, 18, 24, 30).

## 6. Publishing SOP

Order is fixed, every episode:
1. **Source verified.** The actual study is located and read, citation confirmed. No episode is scripted from a secondhand claim.
2. **Render on the no cost engines** (Remotion via GitHub Actions, zero cost) with the standard opener and close.
3. **Private render lands in Google Drive** in the Elements of Power folder, named per convention: NBM_ElementsPower_[Element]Part[N]_YYYY-MM-DD_HHmmCT.
4. **Lizzy approval.** Lizzy watches the Drive render and gives the word. Nothing posts without it.
5. **Post** with the description template below, add to the element playlist, pin the comment, verify it is live.
6. **Log it** the same session: feature registry, THE LEDGER, and the content calendar row marked posted.

## 7. Longer Form Evolution Path

Shorts first, always, until traction (working gate: 1,000 subscribers or a clear breakout episode with strong average view duration).

Then, in order:
1. **Element compilations (3 to 5 minutes):** stitch each finished 6 part series into one longform piece per element. Near zero new production cost, tests longform appetite.
2. **Cinematic element films (8 to 12 minutes):** one flagship per element, full mystical sci-fi treatment, the heroine's journey through that element, deeper dives into 3 or 4 studies each. Built on the same no cost engines, rendered in cinematic pacing.
3. **The Activate documentary (20 plus minutes):** the whole 30 day arc as one piece, viewer declarations woven in, the journal as the companion.
4. **Second season decision point:** either deepen (Part 7 plus for each element with new studies) or widen (element pairings, like water and fire together), based on which series' watch data leads.

Longform unlocks mid roll potential and channel authority; shorts stay the discovery engine throughout. No longform production starts before the 30 episode shorts arc is complete and approved.
